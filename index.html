About Dr. Barseghyan

Dedicated to providing exceptional dermatological care

Professional Biography

Dr. Lusine Barseghyan is a board-certified dermatologist and cosmetologist with over 15 years of experience. After completing her medical education at Yerevan State Medical University, she specialized in dermatology and aesthetic medicine, dedicating her career to helping patients achieve healthy, radiant skin.

Dr. Barseghyan combines evidence-based medicine with the latest aesthetic techniques. Her expertise spans from treating medical skin conditions to advanced cosmetic procedures including Botox, dermal fillers, HydraFacial, chemical peels, and mesotherapy. She takes time to understand each patient's concerns and develops personalized treatment plans.

Areas of Specialization

  • Medical Dermatology
  • Injectable Treatments (Botox & Fillers)
  • Facial Rejuvenation & Anti-Aging
  • HydraFacial & Chemical Peels
  • Chronic Skin Condition Management
  • Pediatric Dermatology

Patient Care Philosophy

Dr. Barseghyan believes in building lasting relationships with her patients based on trust, transparency, and open communication. She takes time to thoroughly explain diagnoses and treatment options, empowering patients to make informed decisions about their skin health. Patient safety and comfort are always the top priorities in her practice.

Dermatological Services

Comprehensive skin care for patients of all ages

Medical Dermatology

Diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions including acne, eczema, psoriasis, dermatitis, rosacea, and skin infections. Using evidence-based approaches to restore skin health.

Suitable for: Adults and children experiencing persistent or acute skin conditions

Injectable Treatments

Botox, Dysport, and dermal fillers for wrinkle reduction, facial contouring, and lip enhancement. Natural-looking results with minimal downtime.

Suitable for: Adults seeking non-surgical facial rejuvenation and anti-aging treatments

Pediatric Dermatology

Gentle, specialized care for children's skin conditions including eczema, birthmarks, warts, and childhood rashes. Creating a comfortable environment for young patients.

Suitable for: Infants, children, and adolescents with skin concerns

Preventive Skin Care

Personalized consultations focused on maintaining skin health, sun protection guidance, and establishing effective skincare routines tailored to your skin type.

Suitable for: Anyone seeking to maintain healthy skin and prevent future conditions

Chronic Condition Management

Long-term management plans for chronic skin conditions such as psoriasis, eczema, and vitiligo. Regular monitoring and treatment adjustments for optimal control.

Suitable for: Patients requiring ongoing care for persistent skin conditions

Cosmetic Dermatology

Medical-grade cosmetic consultations for concerns such as hyperpigmentation, scarring, and skin aging. Evidence-based treatments with realistic expectations.

Suitable for: Adults seeking medical guidance on cosmetic skin concerns
